01   Business Audit

What's Working — and What's Leaking

Uptown has built something rare: a multi-province med spa franchise with real client loyalty and a sophisticated membership model. The opportunity is automating what's already working so it scales without friction.

✓ Strength

Multi-Location Franchise Model

4 locations across BC and Ontario with a proven playbook. One automation system rolls out across every location — multiplying ROI without multiplying cost.

✓ Strength

VIP Skin Gym Membership

The $199/year VIP base plus unlimited service tiers is a sophisticated recurring revenue structure — but it's sold manually with zero nurture automation.

✓ Strength

15K Engaged Social Following

@skinbyuptown drives real inquiry volume across provinces. That warm audience just needs a system to capture and convert them after hours.

✓ Strength

Named Technician Loyalty

Clients book specifically for Fatemeh, Jamie, and Simran. This loyalty is a retention asset that automation can amplify through personalized follow-ups.

⚠ Gap

Zero Cross-Location Automation

Each location handles inquiries independently and manually. A Surrey inquiry could be rerouted to Langley — but no system exists to make that happen.

⚠ Gap

Membership Funnel is Broken

VIP sign-up is entirely manual. No automated welcome sequence, no nurture flow, no non-member upgrade prompts. Interested clients go cold.

⚠ Gap

After-Hours Inquiry Black Hole

Laser packages and Morpheus8 are high-consideration purchases researched at night. Without 24/7 coverage, those inquiries land nowhere until morning.

⚠ Gap

No Reactivation System

Laser hair removal requires 8 sessions. Without automated follow-up, clients who lapse after session 3 start over somewhere else.
