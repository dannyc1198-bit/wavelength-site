Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026  —  Fort Langley, BC, Canada

Wavelength Solutions (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website chat widget, contact us, or book a discovery call:

  • Name and email address (when voluntarily submitted through our chat or contact forms)
  • Business name and industry (when you share it during a conversation)
  • Messages and inquiries submitted through our AI chat assistant
  • General usage data (page visits, browser type) collected anonymously

We do not collect payment information directly. Payments are processed through Stripe, which has its own privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule discovery calls
  • Follow up on your interest in Wavelength Solutions services
  • Provide you with information about services relevant to your business
  • Improve our website and chat experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. CASL Compliance

We comply with Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). We will only send you commercial electronic messages if you have given us express or implied consent to do so. Every commercial message we send will include a clear unsubscribe mechanism. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at danciampelletti@wavelengthsolutions.co.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely. We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. Our website is hosted on Netlify and our chat is powered by Claude AI (Anthropic), each with their own security and privacy practices.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Netlify — website hosting and serverless functions
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — powers our chat assistant; messages are processed per Anthropic’s privacy policy
  • Cal.com — online booking for discovery calls
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • VAPI — AI voice assistant demo line

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

7. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. Third-party services embedded in our site (such as Cal.com) may set their own cookies subject to their respective policies.
